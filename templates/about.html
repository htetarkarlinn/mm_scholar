About MM Scholar

A free scholarship finder built specifically for Myanmar students

Why MM Scholar Exists

Finding a scholarship as a Myanmar student is harder than it should be. Information is scattered across hundreds of websites, eligibility requirements are unclear, and many students miss great opportunities simply because they never heard about them.

MM Scholar was built to change that. As a Myanmar student studying abroad through the EU Mobility Programme for Myanmar (EMPM), I know this challenge personally — and I wanted to build something that would make it easier for every Myanmar student who comes after me.

MM Scholar brings together {{ num_scholarships }} verified scholarships from {{ num_countries }} countries into one place, with AI-powered matching that helps you find the right opportunity in seconds — completely free.

How MM Scholar Works

🎯
Step 1
Tell us your goals

Enter your preferred country, study level, and funding type. Add your field and scores for more precise results.

🔍
Step 2
We find your matches

Our system searches {{ num_scholarships }} verified scholarships and ranks the best matches for your profile using machine learning.

💡
Step 3
Understand your options

Click "Why this matches me?" on any result to get a personalised AI explanation of why the scholarship suits you and what you need to apply.
